# 📅 8-Week Typing Improvement Plan

## Week 1: Home Row Mastery
- **Focus:** A S D F — J K L ;
- **Goal:** Type home row letters without looking
- **Daily time:** 15 minutes
- **Exercise:** asdf jkl; asdf jkl; (repeat 50×)

## Week 2: Top Row
- **Focus:** Q W E R T — Y U I O P
- **Goal:** Reach top row without lifting wrists
- **Daily time:** 20 minutes
- **Exercise:** Mix home row + top row words

## Week 3: Bottom Row
- **Focus:** Z X C V B — N M , . /
- **Goal:** Complete alphabet coverage
- **Daily time:** 20 minutes

## Week 4: Common Words
- **Focus:** "the", "and", "for", "you", "are", "but"
- **Goal:** Type 50 most common English words fluently
- **Daily time:** 25 minutes

## Week 5–6: Speed Building
- **Focus:** Increase WPM
- **Method:** 1-minute timed tests, push past comfort zone
- **Goal:** 30 WPM with 90% accuracy

## Week 7: Number & Symbol Row
- **Focus:** 1234567890 !@#$%
- **Daily time:** 20 minutes

## Week 8: Real Text Practice
- **Focus:** Type articles, emails, code
- **Goal:** 40+ WPM on real text
