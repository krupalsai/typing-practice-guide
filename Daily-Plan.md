## Tips for Plateaus

If you're stuck at a WPM for more than a week:

1. **Slow down to 70% of max speed** → focus on accuracy
2. **Identify problem keys** → drill only those 10 min/day
3. **Change practice text** → try code, numbers, or quotes
4. **Check your posture** → tension slows you down

> Remember: Accuracy builds speed. Speed never builds accuracy.
